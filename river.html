Klamath River's Water Quality Exploration

In this USGS project, we investigated Klamath River's thermal stratification by examining weather and river flow data. We employed generalized additive models, random forest models, and K-means clustering to uncover patterns and establish a stratification threshold, guiding water resource management strategies.
