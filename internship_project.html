Maternal Stress Volatility During Pregnancy on Child Health

The study investigates the effects of maternal stress volatility during pregnancy on child health outcomes using modern biostatistics and data science techniques. By analyzing stress fluctuations based on biophysical, contextual, and demographic features, the research explores its impact on infant health.
