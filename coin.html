Coin detection system - Deep learning

  • Click here to read the full report
  • Click here for code

In this innovative project, I collected diverse images of US coins using my smartphone and labeled them with bounding boxes through the LandingLens platform. I independently trained a deep neural network for object detection, evaluating its performance based on data quality. After deploying the model as a web app, the final system detects various US coins in an image.
