Food recommendation system

In this food recommendation system project, I utilized feature engineering and scaling to preprocess nutritional data. By independently implementing K-means clustering and KNN regression algorithms from scratch and comparing them to sklearn's implementation, I successfully created a system that recommends similar food items based on user preferences. Throughout the project, I collaborated closely with my supervisor, ensuring clear communication of both the process and the final results.
